Solid Rock Preschool

To nurture, inspire, and challenge children in an environment that sustains independent thinking, creativity, and respect.

Welcome to Solid Rock Preschool

Welcome to Solid Rock Preschool. We are a Texas Rising Star childcare center for children as young as 6 weeks old to as old as 12 years. We offer school drop-off and pick-up, before and after school care, snacks and meals, transportation to and from school, and summer camp.

When you leave your child in our hands, you can trust that we are nurturing, teaching, and protecting your precious child. Give your child a bright start while you are taking care of your business.

Our Mission

Our mission at Solid Rock Preschool is to provide a safe, stimulating, and supportive learning environment where children can thrive. We are committed to:

  • Fostering a love of learning that will last a lifetime
  • Respecting each child's individual development and learning style
  • Building strong partnerships with families
  • Preparing children for success in school and beyond
  • Creating a community where diversity is celebrated

Our Program

Infant Program

Infants thrive in an environment where they feel safe and secure. At Solid Rock Preschool, we provide an age-appropriate play environment designed to foster movement, exploration, and communication. Our program promotes social, emotional, and language development, guided by nurturing teachers. Using music, movement, and songs, we stimulate their senses, encouraging their growth every step of the way.

Through a process of assessment, planning and evaluation and information sharing with parents, we are able to implement a collaborative learning programme that is in-depth and relevant.

Toddler Program

Our toddler program introduces your child to the basic elements of early learning such as visual recognition of letters, colors, numbers, shapes, songs, patterns and a healthy social environment while building self-awareness, self-expression and confidence. Through both structured and unstructured play, our teachers help develop toddlers' gross motor and social skills.

We create opportunities for toddlers to explore, discover, and express themselves in a safe, supportive setting where every milestone is celebrated.

Preschool Program

Pre-School is a vital building block to kindergarten success.

We guarantee that children achieve significant development advancements, learn to communicate effectively, and build collaborative skills, by working independently and in groups through a robust early childhood education approach. We ensure that children are exposed to diverse learning activities and help them to thrive in all areas of child development, problem solving and creative thinking.
