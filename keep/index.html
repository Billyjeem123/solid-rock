About Us

Washington Academy is a college preparatory school dedicated to academic excellence and character development. Our faculty and staff are committed to helping students reach their full potential in a supportive and challenging environment.

Our campus features state-of-the-art facilities including science laboratories, a performing arts center, athletic fields, and a modern library.

Academic Programs

Our comprehensive curriculum includes:

  • Advanced Placement courses
  • STEM focus programs
  • Visual and performing arts
  • Foreign language instruction
  • College counseling

Washington Academy maintains a student-to-teacher ratio of 15:1, ensuring personalized attention for all students.
