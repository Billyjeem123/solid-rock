Our Philosophy

Philosophy

At Solid Rock, we have created a unique, homely, and peaceful haven for children. We believe that the strength of our program lies in the dedication of our teaching staff. We support our teachers with training, resources, and the freedom to create a unique learning experience for children. We provide many opportunities for teachers to share ideas and grow professionally. Our teachers create a caring and nurturing atmosphere that helps children function in their unique world. We believe parents are the most significant adults in a child's life. We strive to create mutual respect between parents and teachers, building a partnership for the benefit of the child. Our doors are always open to parents. Our focus remains on providing excellent childcare with the best resources and heartfelt dedication to every child's growth.

Our Mission

Empowering young learners to reach their full potential through creativity and compassion.

Our Vision

To be a leading preschool recognized for excellence in early childhood education and care.

Our Values

Integrity, Respect, Innovation, and Passion guide everything we do at Solid Rock Preschool.
